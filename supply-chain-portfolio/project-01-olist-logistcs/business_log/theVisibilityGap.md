* **Observation**: We encountered "Invalid Values" (NaNs) in the delivery timestamps.

* **Business Impact**: If the system doesn't record when a carrier receives a package, Olist cannot hold that carrier or seller accountable. This is **"Information Muda"** (waste).

* **The Strategy**: As a PM, one of our first "Tech Features" might be Mandatory Handover Scans to ensure 100% data coverage. We can't optimize what we can't see.
