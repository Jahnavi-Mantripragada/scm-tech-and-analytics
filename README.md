# Supply Chain Analytics University

A self-directed curriculum and project lab for learning Supply Chain Management with Data Science, Machine Learning, and Optimization. This repository collects notes, experiments, models, simulations, and analyses that connect theoretical SCM concepts with practical computational approaches and real-world business insight.

## Tracks

0. Python & Tools Refresh
1. Foundations
2. Demand Forecasting
3. Inventory & Replenishment
4. Network & Logistics Optimization
5. Simulation & Risk
6. Retail & Pricing Analytics
7. Product & Systems Thinking

This repository will grow incrementally as I add new notebooks, insights, and explorations.
